About Me

I am a results-driven Software Engineer with a strong background in software quality assurance and web development. With a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from East West University, I have honed my technical expertise and problem-solving skills through hands-on experience in various industries. My professional journey has equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of modern development frameworks, database management, and version control systems.

I am proficient in multiple programming languages, including C, C++, PHP, C#, and JavaScript, and have extensive experience with frameworks such as Laravel, Vue.js, and ASP.NET. My technical toolkit also includes expertise in API development, jQuery, Ajax, and modern CSS frameworks like Tailwind CSS and Bootstrap. Additionally, I am skilled in managing databases using MySQL, pgSQL, and Oracle, and I maintain code integrity through version control systems like Git, GitHub, and GitLab.

I thrive in collaborative environments, consistently ensuring that projects meet and exceed quality standards. My commitment to continuous learning and passion for technology drive me to stay updated with industry trends, enabling me to deliver innovative and effective solutions.

My Services

Backend Development

With hands-on experience in various programming languages and frameworks such as PHP, Laravel, ASP.NET, and C#, I create dynamic, scalable, and secure web applications tailored to meet client needs. My work includes developing complete solutions from backend logic to user interface design.

SQA

As a Software Engineer in SQA, I ensure that web projects meet the highest standards of quality. My role involves writing effective test cases, identifying and fixing bugs, and validating that every product aligns with client specifications, ensuring a flawless user experience.

Frontend Development
